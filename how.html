Platform Walkthrough

How FounderVest Works

A simple, transparent process that connects founders and investors without gatekeeping or unnecessary complexity.

For Founders
From signup to funded

Every step is designed to reduce friction and build trust with investors.

1
📝
Create your startup profile

Sign up free and fill in your startup details — name, sector, stage, city, funding ask, and a short pitch. This becomes your public-facing profile that investors browse.

Takes less than 15 minutes to complete
No pitch deck required to get started
You control what's visible publicly vs. privately
2
🛡️
Get verified

Our team reviews your MCA registration, GST details, LinkedIn profiles, and startup basics. Verified startups get a credibility badge that makes investors take you seriously from the first look.

Verification takes 2–3 business days on average
We check: MCA/company registration, team background, pitch validity
Verified badge is displayed prominently on your profile
Idea-stage startups can also get verified with team & concept review
3
🤖
Get matched by AI

Our matching engine analyses your sector, stage, location, and funding ask against active investor profiles to suggest the most relevant matches — no cold emailing needed.

Matches are based on investor-declared preferences
You'll see why each match was suggested
Accept or decline match suggestions anytime
4
🤝
Connect and have conversations

Once both sides accept a match, you can message directly on the platform. Schedule intro calls, share additional documents, and answer investor questions — all in one place.

In-platform secure messaging
Document sharing with access controls
Meeting scheduling support (coming soon)
5
⚖️
Legal & deal support

When you're ready to close, we connect you with legal partners for term sheet review, shareholder agreements, and compliance — so you don't sign something you don't understand.

Term sheet templates and review
Shareholder agreement guidance
MCA filing and compliance support
Success fee of 2–5% applies only on closed deals
For Investors
Discover and invest with confidence

We do the filtering so you can focus on the decisions that matter.

📋

Set your investment preferences

Tell us your sectors of interest, preferred stage, ticket size, and geography. This powers your personalised deal flow.

  • → Sector focus (Fintech, EdTech, AgriTech, etc.)
  • → Investment stage (Idea, MVP, Revenue, Growth)
  • → Ticket size range
  • → Geography (Pan-India or specific states)
🤖

Receive curated deal flow

Our AI surfaces verified startups that match your criteria — saving you hours of sifting through irrelevant pitches.

  • → AI-filtered matches based on your preferences
  • → Only verified startups appear in your feed
  • → Email alerts for new high-match startups
  • → Weekly digest of top deal opportunities
📊

Review structured profiles

Every startup profile includes standardised data fields so you can compare opportunities apples to apples.

  • → Team background and LinkedIn verification
  • → Revenue or traction metrics (where available)
  • → Pitch deck access (after match accepted)
  • → Funding history and current cap table (on request)
🚀

Close and track

Once you decide to invest, we support the deal close and let you track your portfolio in one place.

  • → Deal room for secure document exchange
  • → Legal support for term sheets
  • → Portfolio dashboard (coming soon)
  • → Follow-on opportunity alerts
