My Story

Hi, I'm Ujjawal.
I built this for all of us.

I'm a student, and I don't come from a startup hub. I don't have an IIT alumni network, a family business to fall back on, or a childhood friend who works at a VC fund. What I do have is a very clear picture of the problem — because I've lived close to it.

Across India, there are hundreds of thousands of people with real startup ideas — practical, grounded ideas that could improve agriculture, healthcare, education, and finance for millions. But the vast majority of them never get funded. Not because their ideas are bad. Because they don't know the right people.

The Indian startup ecosystem has a geography problem. Capital flows disproportionately to founders in Bangalore, Mumbai, and Delhi — specifically to people who went to the right colleges or moved in the right circles. A founder from Mathura, Gorakhpur, Ranchi, or Raipur faces a completely different starting line. That's not a meritocracy. It's a network game — and it's broken.

FounderVest is my attempt to fix this. I want to build a platform where a first-generation founder from a Tier 4 city can walk in, get verified, get matched with the right investors, and get the same shot as someone from a top-20 college with three warm intros already lined up.

We're just getting started. There are no thousands of users, no VC backing, no famous advisors. It's just me, a clear mission, and a genuine belief that this platform can change who gets to build the next generation of Indian companies.

If you're a founder who's been overlooked — join us. If you're an investor who wants to find deals before the crowd does — join us.

We're building this together. From scratch. Honestly.
